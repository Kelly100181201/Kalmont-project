Services & Support

Effective avalanche rescue involves advanced data analysis, digital equipment, and well-trained search dogs. Rescue teams employ specialized tools, utilize helicopter support in remote areas, and include medical personnel for swift and comprehensive operations. Collaboration with local authorities and volunteers enhances support and manpower, ensuring efficient and successful rescue missions.

Rescue Tools

Our rescue teams employ a range of specialized tools designed for swift and effective search and rescue operations, particularly in avalanche-prone areas.

Avalanche Shovels

Specially designed shovels are essential for quickly and efficiently digging through snow to reach buried individuals. Their ergonomic design and durability ensure effectiveness in challenging conditions.

Probes

Rescue teams use probes to penetrate the snow and pinpoint the exact location of buried individuals. These lightweight and portable devices aid in rapidly assessing the depth of the snowpack.

Helicopter Support

In remote or inaccessible areas, where traditional search methods may be challenging, we leverage helicopter support. Helicopters enable our teams to cover vast terrains quickly, reaching difficult-to-access locations and enhancing the overall efficiency of our rescue efforts.

Probe Poles

These extended poles with specialized tips assist in probing snow-covered areas to detect buried individuals. Probe poles are valuable for assessing snowpack stability and locating victims in a timely manner.
