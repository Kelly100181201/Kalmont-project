Services & Support

Effective avalanche rescue involves advanced data analysis, digital equipment, and well-trained search dogs. Rescue teams employ specialized tools, utilize helicopter support in remote areas, and include medical personnel for swift and comprehensive operations. Collaboration with local authorities and volunteers enhances support and manpower, ensuring efficient and successful rescue missions.

Digital Equipment

Our rescue team is equipped with state-of-the-art digital devices crucial for efficient search and rescue operations. The following are key digital tools we utilize:

Avalanche Sensors

These advanced sensors monitor real-time avalanche activity in the vicinity. Employing precision technology, they promptly send alerts upon detecting avalanche risks, providing valuable warning time for our rescue teams to respond effectively.

Rescue Beacons

Individuals in distress carry rescue beacons emitting specific signals. These small devices allow our equipment to precisely locate the missing persons, proving essential for rapid searches in vast snow-covered areas.

Transceivers

Rescue team members are equipped with high-performance transceivers, ensuring real-time and reliable communication during search and rescue operations. This aids coordination within the team and facilitates communication with other relevant units.
