Services & Support

Effective avalanche rescue involves advanced data analysis, digital equipment, and well-trained search dogs. Rescue teams employ specialized tools, utilize helicopter support in remote areas, and include medical personnel for swift and comprehensive operations. Collaboration with local authorities and volunteers enhances support and manpower, ensuring efficient and successful rescue missions.

Search Dogs

Our team benefits from the exceptional skills of well-trained search dogs, enhancing the efficiency of our search and rescue operations, especially in challenging snowy terrains.

Highly Trained Canines

Our search dogs undergo rigorous training to develop their keen sense of smell and specialized skills. This allows them to locate individuals buried beneath the snow with remarkable accuracy.

Enhanced Search Efficiency

The use of search dogs significantly expedites the search process. Their ability to cover large areas quickly and detect scents inaccessible to human senses makes them invaluable assets in locating missing individuals, especially in avalanche-prone regions.

Precise Location Detection

Search dogs are trained to pinpoint the exact location of buried individuals, facilitating the rapid response of our rescue teams. This precision is crucial in optimizing the efficiency and effectiveness of our search and rescue efforts.
