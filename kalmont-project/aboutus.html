Established in 2008, our organization excels in high-mountain rescues, completing over 1000 missions with real-world expertise. Through advanced training, technology, and teamwork, we ensure optimal responsiveness and safety. Our commitment extends to continuous improvement, updating programs, and fostering trust. With each successful rescue, we take pride in providing the highest safety standards for mountain enthusiasts, ensuring a secure and confident mountain experience.

Organizational Structure

Our organizational structure prioritizes efficiency and collaborative teamwork. It comprises rescue teams, training departments, logistical support, and communication coordination. Each department is staffed with experienced professionals, working collectively to achieve organizational goals and missions.

Rescue Team

Our highly skilled Rescue Team, led by the Rescue Captain, specializes in executing daring mountain rescues. Team members undergo rigorous training, ensuring they are well-prepared to navigate challenging terrains, providing swift and efficient assistance to those in need.
