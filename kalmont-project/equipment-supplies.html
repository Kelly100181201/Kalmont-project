Equipment and Supplies

When engaging in mountain activities, it is vital to carry essential equipment for safety and preparedness. Our recommendations include a detailed list covering items such as emergency cold-weather clothing, food, water, navigation tools, and more. To ensure your safety, we provide guidelines on the correct usage and maintenance of this equipment, offering valuable insights on maximizing their effectiveness in diverse mountain environments.

Food

  • High-energy snacks

  • Portable, non-perishable meals

  • Electrolyte-rich beverages

Water

  • Hydration reservoir or water bottles

  • Water purification tablets or filters

Navigation Tools

  • Map and compass

  • GPS device

  • Signal mirror

  • Whistle

First Aid Kit

  • Bandages and gauze

  • Antiseptic wipes

  • Pain relievers

  • Personal medications

Emergency Shelter

  • Lightweight tent or space blanket

Illumination

  • Headlamp or flashlight

  • Extra batteries

Communication Devices

  • Two-way radios

  • Personal locator beacon (PLB)

Multi-Tool

  • Swiss army knife or multi-tool
